The Mobile Solution

Leaving Your Pet in a Salon Causes Massive Anxiety.

As certified technicians we often see pets developing severe anxiety due to traditional grooming shop visits. Dropping off your pet for an all day ordeal in a loud chaotic environment filled with barking dogs is detrimental to their well being.

Over time this causes your dog to associate grooming with extreme fear rather than relief. If left unaddressed grooming becomes a traumatic event.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our mobile grooming business in Orange County? We have the technical answers.

How does mobile grooming save me time?
Traditional salon appointments are an all day ordeal. We eliminate the commute the drop off line and the hours of waiting. We come directly to you saving you travel time and stress.
Do I need to provide water or power?
No. Our mobile grooming vans are completely self contained with onboard fresh water tanks water heaters and power generators. We arrive ready to pamper.
Is this better for anxious dogs?
Absolutely. We eliminate car anxiety separation anxiety from being left in a shop all day and stress caused by other barking dogs. It is a quiet environment.
Can I stay in the van during the groom?
For safety and the calm execution of our specialized technical process pet parents are not permitted to stay inside the van. Your pet needs maximum focus on the technician.
