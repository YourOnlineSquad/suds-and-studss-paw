The Mobile Advantage

Traditional Salons Are Stressing Out Your Pet.

A common issue we see with pet owners in Orange County is dogs developing severe anxiety related to grooming. The process of being forced into a car, dropped off in a loud room filled with barking dogs, and left in a cage for hours takes a heavy toll.

Over time, this causes your dog to associate grooming with fear rather than relief. If left unaddressed, grooming becomes a traumatic event that risks aggressive behavior or poor hygiene.

The Suds & Studs Solution:

We eliminate the commute and the cages. By bringing our climate-controlled mobile spaw to your driveway, your dog receives undivided, one-on-one attention in a quiet, sanitized environment. The outcome? A beautiful, fresh pup who actually looks forward to their spaw day.

Booking Guidelines

Cancellation & No Show Policy

Because our mobile routes are carefully planned to serve Orange County efficiently, last-minute cancellations severely impact our daily operations. We strive to provide the most reliable service possible, which requires clear communication.

Please note our standard spaw policies:

  • Text reminders are sent out at the beginning of the week of your appointment.
  • Cancellation notice is required at least 48 hours in advance.
  • Cancellations with less than 48 hours notice require a $35.00 "No Show" Fee.
    (This fee may be avoided if we are able to fill your time slot last minute!)
  • We provide a 15 minute grace period for all appointments.
  • 2 or more "No Call No Shows" require full payment upfront to book your next appointment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about mobile grooming in Santa Ana? We've got answers.

What is your cancellation policy?
We require 48 hours notice for cancellations. Less than 48 hours notice incurs a $35 No Show fee, unless we can fill the spot. Two no-shows require prepayment for future bookings.
Do I need to provide water or power?
No. Our mobile grooming vans are completely self-contained with onboard fresh water tanks, water heaters, and power generators.
How long does a grooming session take?
Most standard spaw treatments take 1 to 2 hours. Because we offer one-on-one attention without cage drying, the process is efficient and much less stressful for your dog.
Is mobile grooming better for anxious dogs?
Absolutely. We eliminate car ride anxiety, separation anxiety from being left in a shop all day, and stress caused by other barking dogs. It is a quiet, 1-on-1 environment.
Do you require vaccination records?
Yes, for the safety of your pet and our groomers, we require proof of an up-to-date Rabies vaccination prior to your first appointment.
Can I stay in the van during the groom?
For safety reasons and to ensure your pet focuses on the groomer without trying to jump to you, pet parents are not permitted to stay inside the van during the active grooming process.
